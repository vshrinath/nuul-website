Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to be technical to use nuul? ▾
Not at all. Setup takes about ten minutes — connect your GitHub account, get a free API key from Groq, and you're running. The interface is designed for people who think for a living, not engineers. If you can use Google Docs, you can use nuul.
Who can see my data? ▾
Nobody but you. Your vault is stored in a private GitHub repository that you own. nuul never touches your data on our servers — the engine runs on your machine. Even if nuul disappeared tomorrow, your files are yours in plain readable markdown.
Does nuul replace my AI subscription? ▾
No — nuul makes your AI subscriptions dramatically more useful. Think of it as the memory layer that your Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini subscription is missing. nuul passes your accumulated context to whichever model you use, so every session starts with the full picture instead of a blank slate.
What if I want to switch models? ▾
Just change one line in your config. Your entire operational memory — notes, people, threads, decisions — travels to the new model instantly. The context is stored as plain markdown, not inside any AI provider's systems. Switch freely.
How is this different from Notion AI or ChatGPT memory? ▾
Notion AI works on your documents but doesn't compound across time or connect conversations, people, and voice captures. ChatGPT memory is limited, vendor-controlled, and disappears if you switch models. nuul gives you an open, growing, structurally-linked operational memory that works with any model — including ones that don't exist yet.
Does nuul support mobile? ▾
Yes — there's a mobile PWA for quick capture, voice notes, and reviewing your inbox. The full interface is available on desktop via the app. WhatsApp works as a lightweight capture surface from any device.
